## Notes

- **Africa campaigns** run at significantly lower CPM ($1.50–$4 vs $15–$25 for US). High reach, lower conversion value — treat as brand building and long-term market development.
- **Pinterest** has a longer buying cycle than Facebook. Shoppers pin and come back weeks later. Give campaigns at least 6–8 weeks before judging performance.
- **Google PMax** learns from your catalog and purchase history — feed it at least 30 conversions before making optimization decisions.
- **Retargeting** consistently outperforms cold traffic at 3–5x. Always allocate 15–20% of budget to retargeting even at Tier 1.
- Review spend and ROAS weekly. Pause any ad set with spend over $50 and zero conversions.
